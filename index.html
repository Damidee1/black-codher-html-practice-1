My favourite hobby is baking

I started baking at the age of 11, started with little fish rolls which are my favorites and then moved on to baking mini pies. I have since took numerous baking classes since my teen years and i have attended lots of masterclasses as an adult. For anyone reading this, Baking is cheaper than therapy.

Thinking of embracing Baking as a hobby? Here are three qualities of a good baker;

  1. Organizational Skills
  2. Tidiness
  3. Precision
God gives us the ingredients for our daily bread, but he expects us to do the baking! Chip Ingram
